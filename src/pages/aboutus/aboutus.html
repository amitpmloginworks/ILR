About Us

About ILOVEREADING.SG

ILOVEREADING.SG is the publisher of the “i”, “Inspire” and “IThink” magazines, and provides educational and supplementary reading materials to schools.

Our MISSION is to cultivate the passion for reading in each child, and as such, we are constantly looking out for excellent reading materials to serve our local and international communities.

Our VISION is to help each child acquire skills in language and vocabulary through reading and apply these skills in different areas of life.

About READSFEED

READSFEED is the one-stop reading portal where parents, teachers and students can connect to help build the reading community of the future.

READSFEED takes all the things you love about our magazines and brings them online! In an increasingly digital world, ILOVEREADING.SG is taking the next step and making it easier for you to access all or great material in one place.
